VISITOR INFORMATION

Not sure where to begin?


Joining a church for the first time can be a very intimidating experience. At CHT, we always welcome visitors with open arms. Our staff will be there to assist you at the information desk if you have any concerns.


If you have further questions, feel free to contact us by phone or email!


What should I expect?

We give praise to Jesus Christ, our Lord and Saviour, through music, prayer and fellowship. We practice giving and sharing through a weekly offering to support the work of the church.


What should I wear to a Sunday service?

There is no dress code at our church. Wear what is comfortable!


Can I bring my children?

Absolutely! Following the offering in the morning service, children 3-11 years are invited to go upstairs to the multi-purpose room where we've planned Bible lessons, activities, crafts, and a snack just for them! After the service is over, parents are then asked to please pick up their children. Grades 7-9 will have a class in the teen room and children 3 and under are welcome in the Nursery.


How do I get involved with CHT?

There are countless ways you can help! The best way to begin is to ask one of our staff members of the opportunities and places to serve.
