OUR VALUES

PEOPLE

That every person is valuable to God and us.

WORD OF GOD

That the Word of God provides for us direction, the way of salvation and future hope.

GROWTH

That every person should be committed to a lifestyle of spiritual growth in Christ.

SPIRITUAL GIFTS

That every person has been given gifts by God that need to be discovered, developed and deployed.

RELATIONSHIPS

That we should reflect Christ in our relationships by acting in love, encouragement and care.

SERVICE

That our ministry is motivated by our love for God and the needs of humanity.

PRAYER

That we the church must be committed to prayer, both personally and corporately.
